Can your experience in research data management help me promote it better ?
and vice versa ?

The primary aim of this 'open sciency' project is to create short videos everyone can use to raise the scientists' awareness on RDM issues. But to create amazing material, we first gather stories from data managers and scientists about their relation to data management to choose the most representative topics.
